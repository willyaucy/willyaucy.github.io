Net Worth Estimator

How is the number calculated?
N n Net worth at year n
I n Income at year n
E n Expense at year n
G I Annual percentage growth of income
G R Annual percentage return on investment
G Inflation Annual inflation in percentage
N n = N n − 1 × ( 1 + G R 100 ) + I n − 1 − E n − 1
I n = I n − 1 × ( 1 + G I 100 )
E n = E n − 1 × ( 1 + G Inflation 100 )
